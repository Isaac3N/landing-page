A simple bookmark manager

A clean and simple interface to organize your favourite websites. Open a new browser tab and see your bookmarks and watch it load instantly. Try it free.

Features

Our aim is to make it quick and easy to access many of your favourite websites. Your bookmarks sync between your device so you can access them on the go.

Bookmark in one click

Organize your bookmarks however you like. Our simple drag and drop interface gives you complete control over how you manage your favourite sites.

Intelligent Search

Our powerful search feature will help you find saved sites in no time at all. No need to crawl through all your bookmarks.

Share your books

Easily share your bookmarks and collections with others. Create a shareable link that you can send at the click of a button.
